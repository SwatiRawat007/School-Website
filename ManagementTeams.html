THE DIRECTOR

THE MOTHER PRIDE SCHOOL, PATAUDI’S SUCCESS STORY IS A TESTIMONIAL TO THE PASSION, COMMITMENT AND LEADERSHIP OF ITS DIRECTOR, MR. AMIT GUPTA, HE BELIEVES IN GIVING STUDENTS STRONG VALUES ALONG WITH A SET OF WINGS WHICH MAY CARRY THEM FAR AND WIDE. IN HIS OPINION, STUDENTS MUST BE EQUIPPED TO FACE THE CHALLENGES OF THE RAPIDLY CHANGING WORLD. MOTHER’S PRIDE SCHOOL IS AN AMALGAMATION OF COMPETENT TEACHERS, STATE-OF-THE-ART INFRASTRUCTURE AND AN EXPERIENCED AND EFFICIENT ADMINISTRATION. THE SCHOOL CAME INTO EXISTENCE WITH THE VISION OF CREATING A SAGE AND SUPPORTIVE ENVIRONMENT FOR ITS STUDENTS – TO PROVIDE A PERFECT BALANCE OF ACADEMICS, SPORT, ARTISTIC AND SOCIAL OPPORTUNITIES.

IN WORDS OF MR. AMIT GUPTA

Childhood and learning should be a joy and we strive for that because we believe that “LOVE BLOSSOMS HERE”. Education is about developing the minds, hearts and souls of students so that they are free to become their best selves: and the magic is to be found not in any specific curriculum but in our optimism and our readiness to believe in each child.

THE PRINCIPAL

Principal, Mother’s Pride, Pataudi, Ms. Rajni Yadav’s Vivacity and love for being with children helps bring great joy and passion to whatever she undertakes. Ms. Rajni Yadav has rich experience of more than 16 years in school education an academician and administrator. Ms. Rajni Yadav embarked on a mission in year 2003 to support child education.

FROM THE DESK OF PRINCIPAL:

Education is a never-ending process, it is a lifelong journey. The art of school lies in making it simple and easily accessible to its students. Our children are raised with an attitude that they embrace positive changes happening in this fast-changing world and develop the courage to execute ideas for the welfare of society.

ABOUT MOTHER'S PRIDE SCHOOL:

We, at Mother's Pride identify ways by which each child learns. Our facilitators behave like a supporter, helper and partner in their learning process. We welcome healthy new technologies, reflect on our results and consistently strive to upgrade our processes. We rear students who are creatively curious and strive to experiment and innovation new things on both academic and non-academic fronts. Our pedagogy is child-centric with emphases on inquiry and project based learning. We treasure dynamism and deem that one should be well prepared to adapt to the global scenario and work on his learning accordingly. Besides rigorous scholastic programmes, we seek to develop and nurture the different facts of a child. From academic to dance, music and a variety of sports. On the whole I abide with the saying: “Efforts and courage are not enough without purpose and directions.”
